Accounting System

AISO

AISO is an essential accounting system ready for online implementation on the cloud. AISO can be integrated to other front office and back office system developed by Dimata, i.e.

1. Dimata Hanoman : the property management system
2. Dimata ProChain : sales, process chain and inventory management system
3. Dimata Sedana : micro banking management system

You may also request to integrate AISO to your custom system, then AISO has dynamic mapping of transaction to chart of accounts based on your own book keeping rule.
